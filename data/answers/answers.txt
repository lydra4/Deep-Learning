Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies in George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series. He is killed during the event known as the Red Wedding, which takes place in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." The Red Wedding is a massacre orchestrated by the Freys and the Boltons, with the secret support of the Lannisters..
