Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies in the "A Song of Ice and Fire" series by George R.R. Martin. He is killed during the event known as the Red Wedding, which is depicted in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." The Red Wedding is a massacre orchestrated by the Freys and the Boltons, who betray the Starks during a wedding feast..
