Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies in the "A Song of Ice and Fire" series by George R.R. Martin. His death occurs during the event known as the Red Wedding, which takes place in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." The Red Wedding is a massacre orchestrated by the Freys and the Boltons, where Robb, his mother Catelyn, and many of his bannermen are killed..

Does Ned Stark die? - Yes, Ned Stark dies in George R.R. Martin's book "A Game of Thrones," which is the first novel in the "A Song of Ice and Fire" series. He is executed by beheading on the orders of King Joffrey Baratheon..
Did Renly Baratheon claim the Iron Throne? - Yes, Renly Baratheon did claim the Iron Throne. In "A Clash of Kings," the second book of George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series, Renly declares himself king and gathers support, including a large army, to press his claim. He bases his claim on his popularity and the support of several powerful houses, as well as his belief that his older brother Stannis Baratheon, the rightful heir according to strict primogeniture, would not be a suitable king..
Does Sansa Stark marry Tyrion Lannister? - Yes, Sansa Stark does marry Tyrion Lannister in the "A Song of Ice and Fire" book series by George R.R. Martin. Their marriage is arranged by Tywin Lannister as part of his political strategy. However, the marriage is never consummated, and Sansa eventually escapes King's Landing, effectively ending their marriage..
Was Lysa Arryn pushed through the Moon Door? - Yes, Lysa Arryn was pushed through the Moon Door. In George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series, specifically in the book "A Storm of Swords," Lysa Arryn is murdered by Petyr Baelish, also known as Littlefinger. He pushes her through the Moon Door at the Eyrie, leading to her death..
Does Bronn fight for Tyrion in his trial by combat at the Eyrie? - Yes, Bronn fights for Tyrion in his trial by combat at the Eyrie. In "A Game of Thrones," the first book of the "A Song of Ice and Fire" series by George R.R. Martin, Tyrion Lannister is accused of attempting to murder Bran Stark and is imprisoned at the Eyrie. He demands a trial by combat, and Bronn, a sellsword, volunteers to fight as his champion. Bronn wins the duel against Ser Vardis Egen, resulting in Tyrion's release..
Is Robert Baratheon the father of Joffrey Baratheon? - No, Robert Baratheon is not the biological father of Joffrey Baratheon. However, Joffrey is publicly presented as the son of Robert Baratheon and Cersei Lannister, and he is considered a legitimate heir to the throne in the storyline..
Does Sandor Clegane have a fear of fire? - Yes, Sandor Clegane, also known as "The Hound," has a fear of fire. This fear stems from a traumatic incident in his childhood when his brother, Gregor Clegane, pushed his face into a brazier, causing severe burns. This experience left Sandor with both physical and psychological scars, resulting in his deep-seated fear of flames..
Does Davos Seaworth serve Stannis Baratheon? - Yes, Davos Seaworth serves Stannis Baratheon. In George R. R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series, Davos is a loyal supporter and trusted advisor to Stannis Baratheon, who claims the Iron Throne.
