Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies in George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series. He is killed during the Red Wedding, an event orchestrated by the Freys, the Boltons, and Tywin Lannister. This betrayal occurs in the third book, "A Storm of Swords.".
