Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies. In George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series, Robb Stark is killed during the event known as the Red Wedding. This occurs in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." Robb, along with his mother Catelyn Stark and many of his bannermen, is betrayed and murdered at the wedding feast of Edmure Tully and Roslin Frey, orchestrated by House Frey in alliance with House Bolton and House Lannister..
