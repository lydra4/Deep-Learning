Does Robb Stark Die? - Robb Stark, a character from George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series, does die. He is killed during the event known as the Red Wedding, which takes place in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." The Red Wedding is a massacre orchestrated by the Freys and the Boltons, where Robb, his mother Catelyn, and many of his bannermen are murdered..
