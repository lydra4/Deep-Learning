Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies in the "A Song of Ice and Fire" series by George R.R. Martin. He is killed during the event known as the Red Wedding, which takes place in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." During this event, Robb is betrayed and murdered along with his mother, Catelyn Stark, and many of his bannermen..
