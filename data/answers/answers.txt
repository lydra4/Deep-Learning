Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies in the "A Song of Ice and Fire" book series by George R.R. Martin. His death occurs during the infamous event known as the Red Wedding, which is detailed in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." During the wedding feast, Robb, along with his mother Catelyn and several of his bannermen, are betrayed and murdered by the Freys and the Boltons, who conspire with the Lannisters..
