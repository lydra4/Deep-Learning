Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies in George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series. He is killed during the infamous Red Wedding, which occurs in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." The Red Wedding is a massacre orchestrated by the Freys and the Boltons, where Robb, his mother Catelyn Stark, his wife Talisa, and many of his bannermen are betrayed and murdered..
