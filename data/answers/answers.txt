Does Robb Stark Die? - Yes, Robb Stark dies in George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" series. He is killed during the event known as the Red Wedding, which takes place in the third book, "A Storm of Swords." The event is a massacre orchestrated by the Freys and the Boltons, who betray Robb and his followers during a wedding feast..
